Blackdog Consultants

blackdog-consultants.com

web design & front-end, static site web development

Blackdog Consultants is a unique network of training, facilitation and coaching consultants at your fingertips, specialising in the Asia Pacific Market

The client wanted the site to look modern yet quirky and friendly, with big and bold typography style. I worked with an international team for this project. The logo, typography and color palette was developed by the talented Meggy Kawsek who is based in Los Angeles while the video and illustrations were done by Bernie Quah, founder of Sketchpost based in Singapore.

I was part of this project since it started, from creating the wireframe drafts to show the layout until the deployment of the site using Netlify. The most challenging parts of the project were to display the text in a readable manner for both desktop and mobile (yes there's a lot!), as well as the interactive map where every pawprint icon is exactly positioned to the actual location of the country. Updated this 2018, the site uses Netlify CMS, an alternative to a full-blown CMS, as the publishing platform for the news and learning zone articles. With the use of striking stock photos and vivid color combinations, the site turned out pretty awesome.

Golden Horse Medical Supplies

goldenhorsemedicalsupplies.com

front-end development, e-commerce

Golden Horse Medical Supplies is a brick-and-mortar retail shop for various medical supplies specializing in wheelchairs.

For the look of the site, the client wanted to keep the distinct striking red because in Chinese it is a symbol for luck. Based on a generic theme, I modified the stylesheet and image assets such as buttons to fit this color scheme. I also prepared the banner images on the homepage to attract and entice more visitors to go through the site.

The e-commerce store is built on Shopify. I set up the domain, email and other e-commerce store variables. For shipping rates, we use a specific weight-based model to determine the correct shipping rates, especially since their product lines range from small to bulky items. I also instructed their staff on product uploads as well as order management.

Gather Philippines

gatherph.com

front-end development, e-commerce

Gather PH is a brand-house that creatively addresses sustainability and functionality in everyday living. Thus, their tagline, responsible essentials.

The client wanted an e-commerce shop that looks clean and crisp, with a very minimalist branding but at the same time use of colorful imagery for the images to highlight their products. The client is also non-techie, and would like to be able to have the option of altering the site themselves without too much complication. After presenting different options, they found easiest to work with Weebly, with its drag and drop based editor and free SSL. This would be sufficient since they don't plan on having high SKU and inventory levels. The layout and look of the site is based from a generic theme, but I still had to make a few modifications to the main stylesheet and HTML content. I also supported the client in setting up their domain, mail host, as well as showing them how to work with the e-commerce setting of the site.

Raw Bites

rawbites.com.ph

front-end development, e-commerce

Raw Bites is the first healthy snacks subscription box in the Philippines. The site has two functions: manage subscriptions of the monthly box and an online grocery to buy individual items.

The design was made by my business partner who handles creatives. We wanted the site to look fun, fresh and youthful.

The e-commerce shop is built on Shopify. I customized a lot of components and pages on the site using Liquid and JS, for example:

  • Countdown timer (JS) - counts down to the number of days before subscription period expires, and then automatically resets for the following month
  • Landing page sections - enable admin users to change texts, images as needed without the need for code
  • Product subscription page - this concept is entirely new to the market, thus we wanted to present our subscription plans in the easiest understandable way. This custom page lets our customers see all the plans in an organized manner and conveniently select their preferred option.
  • App Integration - we use BOLD apps to handle recurring orders and rewards/loyalty points for our customers.
  • Gift cards - implementation of a different design depending on gift card value, on the product page and PDF voucher emailed to the customer
